Delivery Information

Street-Beast Customs will ship orders to any address in South Africa, orders will only be processed at our Pretoria North Head Office.
All orders and invoices are South African Vat inclusive. You will be responsible for any duties, taxes or similar fees.

♦ Neighboring Countries:
Unfortunately Street-Beast Customs does not arrange any deliveries outside South Africa, you can arrange for your own transport or delivery service to collect your order from one of our dealers.


Courier Costs & Delivery Times

♦ Delivery Times:
Most of our products are available in stock, allowing us to ship orders as soon as payment has been completed. Please allow 5 to 14 working days for delivery (though closer areas usually a lesser delivery time expectancy).
Customers can opt to collect orders directly from Street-Beast Customs. Payment can be online via the website, bank transfer or directly to Street-Beast Customs. Collection can only be made once we have prepared your order.

Refunds & Exchange Policy

Should a product be out of stock, you will be notified and offered a suitable replacement, an option is given to wait a few more days or a complete refund.

All products have a 7 day money back guarantee. Products must not be used at all and proof of purchase (invoice) must be supplied including the original packaging.

Radio's & Amplifiers - Normally these carry warranty, normally 6 months, Street-Beast Customs will send the unit to the manufacturer for a warranty claim, it takes about 10 to 14 working days.
Speakers & Sub-woofers do not have any warranty, unless specifically stated on the product description itself.

NOTE: Any/all Subwoofers, Radios and Ampliers will loose their warranty if not installed by a qualified accredited professional installed.
