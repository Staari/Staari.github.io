DELIVERY INFORMATION

Street-Beast Customs will ship orders to almost any address in South Africa, orders will only be processed at our Pretoria North Head Office.

All orders and invoices are South African Vat inclusive. You will be responsible for any duties, taxes or similar fees.


♦ Neighboring Countries:

Unfortunately Street-Beast Customs does not arrange any deliveries outside South Africa, you can arrange for your own transport or delivery service to collect your order from one of our dealers.


COURIER COST & DELIVERY TIME 

♦ Delivery Times:

Most of our products are available in stock, allowing us to ship orders as soon as payment has been completed.

Please allow up to 5 working days for delivery, though closer areas usually a lesser delivery time expectancy.

Customers can opt to collect orders directly from Street-Beast Customs. Payment can be online via the website, bank transfer or directly to Street-Beast Customs.

Collection can only be made once we have prepared your order.


♦ Courier Costs

Courier costs are determined per weight of items/good:

-2KGs: R70.00
2KGs - 20KGs: R150
Thereafter an additional R10 per KG.
